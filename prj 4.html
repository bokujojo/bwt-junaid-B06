SUPERNATURAL

Supernatural is an anime series that features elements of the paranormal,including ghosts,demons,spirits,mystical powers,psychic abilities , or other unexplained phenomena where characters often battle against supernatural forces or use them to solve mysteries and confront challenges within the story. EXAMPLE : 1. JUJUTSU KAISEN 2. DEATH NOTE 3. ATTACK ON TITAN

HISTORICAL

Historical anime is a series that is set on a specific time period from the past , often based on real historical events , figures, or conflicts , even if the story itself may include fictional elements or embellishments. EXAMPLE:- 1.VINLAND SAGA 2.BERSERK

ROMANCE

A romance anime refers to a type of anime where the3 central plot revolves around the development of a romantic relationship between characters , focusing on their feelings , interactions , and the jjourney towards love. EXAMPLE:- 1. THE FRAGRANT FLOWER BLOOMS WITH THE DIGNITY 2. I WANT TO EAT YOUR PANCREAS

SLICE OF LIFE

A genre that focuses on depicting the everyday lives of characters, showcasing ordinary moments and experiences without major dramatic plotlines or fantastical elements , essentially presenting a "slice " of a normal life. EXAMPLE :- 1. TOKYO GHOUL 2.TOMO CHAN IS A GIRL
